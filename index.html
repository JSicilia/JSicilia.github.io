Projects

Leaps & Bounds

2D Mobile Platformer

Leaps & Bounds is a easy to play 2D platformer. Your aim as the player is to jump up the different platforms to get to the end of the level, simple concept yet the difficulty of these jumps will begin to increase and get more frustrating for the player. The aim for the project was to make something with simple basic controls (you press and hold on either side of the screen) and let the difficulty come from the gameplay.

Cube puzzle game

Mobile puzzle game

Development was first done in 2019 and was put on pause as life got in the way. This project is coming back once Leaps & Bounds has been released. The concept of this game was a 3D Cube in a 2D view that the player could rotate along any axis and the player had to get the smaller cube in the embedded maze to reach the end portal before continuing to the next level. Learnt a lot about level generation when working on this project and I am excited to come back to that with my skills having been improved since then.
